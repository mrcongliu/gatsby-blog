* ###### Next Step: Project Handoff

  In the next section: **From Figma To Website: Building Our Landing Page** we will actually show you how a Developer would take our Figma design and convert it into an actual page using HTML and CSS. There are a few options for you to continue with the course:

  **1.** If you are just curious on how the developer handoff goes and you don't care about HTML and CSS, just watch the **From Figma To Website: Building Our Landing Page** section and you can *ignore all the bonus sections afterwards teaching you HTML and CSS.*

  **2.** If you want to learn a bit of HTML and CSS, then I recommend you go do all of the bonus sections at the end in order, and once done, go to **From Figma To Website: Building Our Landing Page** and *follow along and see if you can* *build the page together!*
